Top Queries

Why are there different prices for the same product? Is it legal?
Glamora is an online marketplace platform that enables independent sellers to sell their products to buyers. The prices are solely decided by the sellers, and Glamora does not interfere in the same. There could be a possibility that the same product is sold by different sellers at different prices...
Can I cancel my order after placing it?
Yes, you can cancel your order before it is shipped. Go to "My Orders" and click on "Cancel Order" next to the item you want to cancel.
What is the return policy of Glamora?
Glamora offers a 7-day return policy for most products. The item should be unused and in original packaging with tags.
How do I track my order?
Once your order is shipped, you will receive a tracking link via SMS and email. You can also check the tracking status in "My Orders".
Which payment methods are accepted?
We accept credit/debit cards, net banking, UPI, PhonePe, Paytm, and cash on delivery for select locations.
I forgot my password. How do I reset it?
Click on “Forgot Password” on the login page and follow the instructions to reset your password via email or OTP.
Can I change my delivery address after placing an order?
Unfortunately, once the order is confirmed, the delivery address cannot be changed. You can cancel and place a new order with the correct address.
Do you offer international shipping?
Currently, Glamora ships only within India. We do not offer international delivery at this time.
What if I receive a damaged or wrong product?
If you receive a damaged or incorrect item, please initiate a return from "My Orders" and we will arrange a pickup and replacement/refund.
How will I detect fraudulent emails/calls seeking sensitive information?
If you receive an email or a call claiming to be from Glamora seeking confidential information like card details, do not respond. Glamora never asks for such details. Report any such incident to law enforcement immediately.
Here are a couple of baits fraudsters often use to cheat consumers:
- Congratulations! You have been nominated as a ‘Top Glamora customer’...
- You have won a lucky draw coupon. Please share your card number to claim...
How will I identify a genuine appointment letter?
Beware of job scams asking for money. Glamora never asks for processing, security, or recruitment fees. All official communication comes only from Glamora email IDs.
Why will 'My Cashback' not be available on Glamora?
'My Cashback' is being replaced by PhonePe. To use PhonePe balance on Glamora, make sure your PhonePe account is active and verified.
